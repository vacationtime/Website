Pillars of Creation
These are pillars of cool molecular hydrogen and dust that are quite large. The left one is 4 light-years long and it's being here evaporated by other stars that are closish. There's little tentacles at the top of the left one that are the size of our solar system and they are collapsing on themselves making new stars. This might not last but as long as those collapses keep drawing mass they will grow. Seems like they're already gone though because of a shockwave produced by a super nova. RIP on space dust.
